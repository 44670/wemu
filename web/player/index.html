Add wemu to Home Screen

On iPhone and iPad, wemu runs from the Home Screen app view.

  1. 1. Open the browser menu Tap the "..." button in the lower-right corner. Use the toolbar button the arrow points to.
  2. 2. Tap the Share button Look for the square with an upward arrow.
  3. 3. Choose Add to Home Screen Then open wemu from the new Home Screen icon.
